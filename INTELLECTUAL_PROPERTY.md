- Do not remove third-party notices or attribution where required.

## 4. Contribution IP Rules

By submitting a contribution, you represent that:

- You own the contribution or have rights to submit it under repository terms.
- The contribution does not knowingly infringe third-party copyright, trademark, or patent rights.
- You agree the contribution is licensed under this repository's license.

Maintainers may request additional proof or a separate CLA for substantial or high-risk contributions.

## 5. Patent and Sensitive Claims

- Do not submit code/materials that are knowingly patent-encumbered without disclosure.
- If your contribution may involve patent claims, disclose this in the PR description.

## 6. Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, reject, or request replacement of content that creates IP risk, including:

- Unlicensed code copies
- Misused trademarks and logos
- Missing attribution or uncertain ownership
